my journey

From premed to engineer

How a biochemistry student headed for medicine found his way into software — and stayed.

Hi — I'm Dushan (also Michael). I'm a full-stack engineer with a strong foundation in web development, built through Hack Reactor's 16-week immersive program. There I shipped several full-stack applications with modern tools, focusing on clean architecture, scalability, and the people using the product.

Before tech, I studied biochemistry and planned to become a doctor. That path taught me to think critically, solve problems under pressure, and stay persistent through hard stretches. I bring that same discipline to engineering — I'd rather understand code fully than ship something I can't explain.

I'm looking to join a collaborative team that values mentorship, growth, and curiosity. I'm early in this chapter, but I improve fast, I care about doing things well, and my goal is simple: to become a dependable engineer who adds lasting value to the product and the people around me.

Curious about anything here? I'd love to talk — reach me from the home page.
